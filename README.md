# SENTIMENT-ANALYSIS-ON-PRODUCT-REVIEW
In today’s digital marketplace, customers actively express their opinions about products and services through online reviews on e-commerce platforms such as Amazon, Flipkart, and Kaggle datasets. These reviews contain valuable information that can help businesses understand customer satisfaction, product performance, and overall brand perception. However, manually analyzing thousands of customer reviews is both time-consuming and inefficient. As the volume of user-generated content continues to grow, automated techniques are required to extract meaningful insights from large datasets.
Sentiment Analysis, a key area of Natural Language Processing (NLP), offers an effective solution by automatically identifying the emotional tone or sentiment expressed in textual data. It classifies opinions into categories such as positive, negative, or neutral, enabling organizations to quickly understand public perception about their products or services. This project, titled “Sentiment Analysis on Product Reviews,” focuses on building an intelligent system capable of analyzing customer reviews and classifying them based on sentiment polarity.
The project integrates both Machine Learning (ML) and Deep Learning (DL) techniques to effectively process and analyze textual data. Initially, the review dataset undergoes several preprocessing steps, including text cleaning, tokenization, removal of stop words, and transformation into numerical representations using techniques such as TF-IDF or embeddings. These steps help convert raw textual information into a structured format suitable for machine learning models.
To evaluate different approaches for sentiment classification, multiple models are implemented and compared. Traditional machine learning algorithms such as Logistic Regression and Naive Bayes are used due to their efficiency and strong performance in text classification tasks. In addition, the project leverages RoBERTa, a transformer-based deep learning model known for its advanced contextual understanding of language. By utilizing this pre-trained language model, the system can capture deeper semantic relationships within the text and achieve improved classification accuracy.
The primary objective of this project is to develop a reliable sentiment classification model that can automatically interpret customer feedback at scale. By analyzing user reviews effectively, businesses can identify strengths, detect recurring issues, and make data-driven decisions to enhance product quality and customer satisfaction.
Overall, this project demonstrates how modern NLP techniques combined with machine learning and deep learning models can transform unstructured customer feedback into actionable insights, supporting organizations in understanding customer opinions and improving their products and services.
